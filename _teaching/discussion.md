Traditionally, course discussions have not been very useful, usually serving as supplemental review
for students who were both struggling and willing to dedicate more time. My professor tasked me
to develop original content and revamp discussion to better facillitate student success within the course.

This entails generating slide decks full of original content that extends beyond lectures, recorded walkthroughs
for past exams and practice questions, and creating worksheets to acclimate students to writing coherent code 
without having to see it run.

With complete creative freedom, discussion content was written and developed by me while worksheets were co-developed
with a peer.
