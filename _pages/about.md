Greetings! I'm a third year student at the University of California, San Diego, majoring in data science and minoring in design. I have a passion for the creative and analytical aspects of data science, and I believe that combining the two can lead to powerful insights and impactful results.

Throughout my academic journey, I've gained valuable experience in data analysis, statistical modeling, and machine learning. I've completed several projects that explore data across multiple domains, from power outages to faking a commentator. In addition to my technical skills, I've honed my design skills through coursework and personal projects. My goal is to create visually appealing data visualizations that communicate insights in a way that is both informative and engaging.

I've also had the opportunity to gain professional experience as a data engineer intern at <a href='https://www.harborcapital.com/'>Harbor Capital</a>. In this role, I worked on a project that involved AWS, SQL, powerBI, and Tableau. This experience allowed me to apply my skills to real-world datasets and taught me the importance of effectively communicating findings to stakeholders.

Through my personal website, I hope to showcase my skills, experiences, and projects to potential employers and collaborators. I'm dedicated to using my expertise to make a positive impact in the world, and I believe that data science and design can be a powerful combination. Thanks for visiting my site!
